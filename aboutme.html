about me

Who is the creative mind behind Mithra.ka Photography? Mithra, a Kanyakumari native, has always been captivated by the beauty of the natural world. With a deep love for art and an eye for detail, Mithra turned a hobby into a profession. The journey from an amateur photographer to a professional was filled with learning, experimenting, and perfecting the craft.

Mithra.ka Photography specializes in a variety of photography styles to cater to diverse client needs. Whether it's wedding photography, portrait sessions, landscape photography, or event coverage, Mithra brings a unique touch to each project. The signature style of Mithra.ka Photography is characterized by vibrant colors, natural lighting, and capturing candid moments that tell a story.

  • Worked with renowned clients
  • Received awards for excellence in wedding photography
